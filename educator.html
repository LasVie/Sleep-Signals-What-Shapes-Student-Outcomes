Interpretation note

What the ranking means

Positive Spearman ρ means worse factor levels line up with worse outcome levels after ordinal coding. Negative values mean the factor moves in a protective direction.

Limitation note

What the heatmap does not prove

These rows do not show causal flow. Reverse causality is plausible, especially for stress and sleep-related strain. The chart shows association inside this sample only.
