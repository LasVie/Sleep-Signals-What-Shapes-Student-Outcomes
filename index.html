Design commitments

What changed from the archived prototype

Bigger type, fewer focal factors, clearer definitions, and a deliberate shift from broad exploration to guided storytelling.

Interpretation guardrails

What this site should not imply

Reverse causality is possible. High stress may worsen sleep, while poor sleep may worsen performance. This dataset is useful for pattern-finding, not diagnosis.

Sleep Signals uses the local survey dataset in data/, plus a bundled benchmark dataset for cross-country context. The archived prototype remains available in prototype/.
